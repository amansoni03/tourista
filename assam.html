⭐ Top Attractions in Assam

From the world's largest river island to dense jungles

🦏 Wildlife & National Parks

🦏 Kaziranga National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage site, home to two-thirds of the world's one-horned rhinoceroses. Elephant and jeep safaris are unforgettable.

UNESCO Site Rhinos Safari

🐅 Manas National Park

A Project Tiger reserve and elephant reserve at the foothills of the Himalayas, known for its rare golden langur.

TigersBiodiversityHimalayan Foothills

🦏 Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary

Often called 'Mini Kaziranga,' it has the highest density of one-horned rhinos in the world.

High DensityBird WatchingGuwahati Nearby

🏝️ The Mighty Brahmaputra

🌅 Majuli Island

The world's largest river island, a hub of Assamese neo-Vaishnavite culture with ancient 'Satras' (monasteries).

River IslandCultureSatras

🛳️ Brahmaputra River Cruise

Experience the soul of Assam on luxury or local ferries, witnessing life along the riverbanks and stunning sunsets.

LuxurySunsetViews

🐒 Umananda Island

The smallest inhabited river island in the world, featuring the Umananda Temple and Golden Langurs, accessible by boat from Guwahati.

Peacock IslandTempleGuwahati

🕉️ Temples & Ahom Heritage

🔱 Kamakhya Temple

One of the oldest and most revered Shakti Peethas in India, situated atop the Nilachal Hill in Guwahati.

Shakti PeethPilgrimageAmbubachi Mela

🏰 Sivasagar (Sibsagar)

The historic capital of the Ahom Kingdom. Home to the towering Sivadol, Rang Ghar (amphitheater), and Talatal Ghar palace.

Ahom HistoryPalacesArchitecture

🕌 Hajo

An ancient pilgrimage center where Hindus, Buddhists, and Muslims converge, featuring the Hayagriva Madhava Temple and Powa Mecca.

Multi-FaithAncientPilgrimage
